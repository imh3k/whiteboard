Head-to-head battle of coin flip strategies

Statistics

Consider a fair coin and two players Alice and Bob. They flip a coin N-times. Alice gets a point for each pair of two consecutive heads, including cases where heads overlap (i.e., HHH yields two points), and for each consecutive pairs of heads followed by tails, Bob earns points. The winner is a player who scored more points. On average, who wins more games? What are the respective win rates and how much percent of games ends in a draw?
